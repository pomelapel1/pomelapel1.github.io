Consecration of Children to the Pierced Hearts of Jesus and Mary

Most Sacred Heart of Jesus, have mercy on us.

Immaculate Heart of Mary, pray for us.

Oh Lord, before your Eucharistic Heart, we come today, to consecrate to you (name of children...), through and within the Immaculate Heart of Mary your Mother.

Oh, Immaculate Heart of Mary, sure refuge for all who trust in you; to you we consecrate today (name of children...), entrusted to their loving parents by God our Father.

Oh Mother of the Word Incarnate, you who are aware that the dangers facing children today are ever increasing, we place in your hands the well-being, the growth and the future of (name of children...).

Mother of Love, receive into your arms (name of children...), just as you received Jesus. Envelop them in the clothes of purity, of innocence, and of obedience, and place them in the manger of this family, uniting them in bonds of mutual love, sacrifice, generosity, fidelity and joy.

Mother of Wisdom, you who so lovingly taught the Son of God how to talk, to pray and to obey, guide (name of children...), through the loving hands of their parents, into a true understanding of faith, of hope and of wisdom. Keep them ever at your side, faithfully leading them into the fullness of Godâ€™s will for them.

Mother most Powerful, you who did lead the Child Jesus into Egypt, away from the dangers facing Him, protect and defend (name of children...), from the world, from the enemy and from sin. Keep them safe from all danger, both physical and spiritual.

By the union of the Immaculate Heart of Mary with the Sacred Heart of Jesus, we pray that (name of children...), consecrated now to the Pierced Hearts, may always live, -in the heart of their family-, in love, peace, joy and fidelity to God. May the Two Hearts reign in their hearts and in their home. Amen.

Daily Prayer for Vocations

Loving God, the provider of all our needs, we, your faithful people, ask that you bless the world and all the Dioces with an increase of men and women for priestly and religious consecrated ministry.

Provide from among us those who will lead and work with us in priestly and consecrated service.

As you call, so may you also provide the context for listening so that they may respond with generosity and joy.

Father, in the power of the Holy Spirit, You send us forth to build up Your kingdom. We pray especially for those whom You are calling to serve the church as priests, deacons, brothers and sisters. Help them to hear and answer Your call to discipleship.

Bless those who are serving now with courage and perseverance. Grant that many will be inspired by their example and faith.

Form our hearts so that we may be a people given to Gospel living and generous for Christian service. Help us all to discern and pursue our vocation in life with strength and courage, making of us a priestly people.

We ask this through Jesus Christ, Your Son, the priest and servant of our hearts. Amen.

Our Lady, Queen of Peace, pray for us!
